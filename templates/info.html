Informational Page - Student EEG based Confusion Classifier



- This is a simple Binary classifier based on Student EEG signals.

- Upload a .csv file with fields - SubjectID, VideoID, Attention,Mediation, Raw, Delta, Theta, Alpha1, Alpha2, Beta1, Beta2, Gamma1, Gamma2.

- A minimum of 72 timesteps is the basic requirement for this classifier.

- The classifier classifies Student as either - Confused or Not Confused along with certain probability.
